Mouse Press/move/cancel/release

If you mouse press on the blob it should go from red to cyan
If you move outside the blob it should go back to red; if you move back in again it should go back to cyan.
Releasing the mouse when the blob is cyan should pop up an alert. This is mouse 'click'

Mouse drag

A press outside of the blob should start a drag; the blob can be dragged anywhere, then released

Console log

All press/drag events are logged to the console

Expected sequences

Everything starts with a Press; no mouse movement leads to an immediate Release.
Press and move inside the blob yields Press Move* Release
Press and move outside the blob yields Press, Press cancel, Drag start, Drag*, Drag End
